About This Project


Welcome to my Game Hub! My name is Deepanshu Pathak, and I'm a first-year B.Tech student. This website is my college project, designed as a fun and interactive platform where you can enjoy various games. Currently, you can try out three exciting games:


  • Memory Puzzle: Test your memory by matching pairs of cards.
  • Whack-a-Mole: Hone your reflexes and see how many moles you can catch!
  • Catch the Egg: Be quick to catch as many falling eggs as possible before they hit the ground.

Through this project, I aimed to apply and enhance my front-end development skills using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I hope you enjoy the experience and have a great time playing!
